Lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Water 2oz.
  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the water hot (boiling)
  2. Insert noodles
  3. Wait for noodles to cook
  4. Once cooked, remove from water and let cool
  5. Put sauce in a baking dish
  6. Add a layer of noodles flat across the bottom of the dish
  7. Add a layer of sauce and cheese on top of noodle layer
  8. Repeat, and add as many noodle/sauce layers as desired
  9. Finally, cover the top with sauce once you have finished layering
  10. Bake in over for 45 mins as 350 degrees
  11. Take out of oven and let cool before enjoying!
